More Than Just a Candle

A warm welcome to the official Yankee Candle Premium Stockist in Bayreuth.

At Yankee Store Bayreuth, we believe in the power of scent to awaken memories, calm the soul, and brighten any home. Our passion for quality fragrance and beautiful design is what drives us every day.

Our Story

2015 Yankee Store Bayreuth opens its doors as a cozy corner shop in the heart of the city.
2018 Recognized as the largest Yankee Candle retailer in Oberfranken.
2022 Expanded to offer over 1,000 products and a unique in-store experience for candle lovers.
Today We are proud to be a local destination for fragrance, warmth, and inspiration.
